Steering Committee

With guidance from the state Legislature, the Washington State Transportation Commission began exploration of RUC in 2012. Together they continue to examine how to successfully transition from the gas tax to a RUC and work to develop recommendations to advance RUC in Washington.

The Steering Committee includes representatives from a variety of organizations, government bodies and industries in Washington, including:

  • Washington State House of Representatives
  • Washington State Senate
  • Puget Sound Regional Council
  • Washington State Department of Transportation
  • Washington State Department of Licensing
  • The Office of State Treasurer
  • Counties and cities
  • Motoring public
  • Auto industry
  • Business
  • Ports
  • Public transportation
  • Environmental interests
  • Trucking industry
  • Consumer groups
