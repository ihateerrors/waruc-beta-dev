FINAL REPORT AND RECOMMENDATIONS ON ROAD USAGE CHARGING SENT TO STATE LEGISLATURE


On January 13, 2020, the Washington State Transportation Commission transmitted their
final report on how Washington can begin a transition away from the state gas tax and toward a road usage
charge system to the Governor, State Legislature and the Federal Highway Administration. Learn more and read the report.
